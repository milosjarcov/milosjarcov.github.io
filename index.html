I'm Milos Jarcov, a CS student at Concordia University in Montreal. I've always been into tech, even as a kid I was the one taking things apart just to see how they worked. That eventually led me to computer science, where I get to dig into everything from low-level C to building stuff with Python and Java.

What really interests me is where tech meets defense. Autonomous systems, AI in aerospace, the kind of engineering that goes into companies like Lockheed Martin and Palantir. That's the direction I want to take my career.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually at the gym, hooping, talking cars, gaming, or figuring out where to travel next. My friends would describe me as someone who stays calm under pressure, thinks logically, and always finds a way to make people laugh.
